Welcome! / ¡Bienvenidos! / ¡Bem-vindos!

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Political Science at Georgia State University.

My areas of specialization include comparative politics and political methodology. My current research focuses on political behavior, social media, populism, and polarization in Latin America, where I apply advanced research methods and Computational Social Science tools to offer insightful responses to significant questions in political science.

On this website, you can find my ongoing research, syllabi of courses I have taught, and CV.

Please feel free to contact me at jgomezcruces1@gsu.edu.
