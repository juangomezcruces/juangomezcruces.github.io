Datasets

Executive Social Media Database

The Executive Social Media Database is a cross-national initiative collecting political executives’ social media discourse and citizen engagement. It includes Twitter/X posts by political leaders and public responses from 30 countries, spanning the years 2014 to 2023. To access the actual data, please contact me.

Gómez Cruces, Juan S. 2023. Executive Social Media Dataset.

Executive Approval Database 3.0

The Executive Approval Database (EAD) 3.0 represents the most comprehensive, publicly available collection of time-series indicators of public support for political executives in the world. It includes annual, quarterly, and monthly versions of the dataset. The annual dataset covers 113 countries with 725,790 input series.

Carlin, Ryan E., Jonathan Hartlyn, Timothy Hellwig, Gregory J. Love, Cecilia Martínez-Gallardo, Matthew M. Singer, Juan S. Gómez-Cruces, and Hilal Sert. 2023. Executive Approval Database 3.0.

Executive Communications Database 1.0

The Executive Communications Database covers the years between 1964 and 2024 in 42 countries with 108,289 communications (speeches, press conferences, press releases, etc) in 23 languages.

Horne, Will, Joshua Allen, Juan S. Gómez-Cruces, Jaime Lindh, Miles Massey, Ryan Carlin, and Cecilia Martinez Gallardo. Forthcoming. Executive Communications Database.
